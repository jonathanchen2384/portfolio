  • Helped develop interactive modules that allow students to inspect abstract math concepts spatially.

About

I am a VR and game developer currently completing my M.S. in Computer Science at San José State University. I enjoy building systems that make complex ideas tangible, especially when software, interaction design, and visual thinking overlap.

My Approach

I approach projects as both an engineer and a maker: prototype the interaction, test the learning flow, refine the visuals, and keep the experience readable for real users. In VR, clarity matters as much as spectacle.

Creative Work

Beyond software, I work across sketching, digital art, ceramics, contemporary design, and Arduino-based experiments. This creative practice gives me a wider toolbox for designing interfaces, objects, and experiences.
